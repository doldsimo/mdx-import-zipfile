<h2>Addition</h2>
<Image data="https://images.pexels.com/photos/374918/pexels-photo-374918.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&dpr=2&h=750&w=1260"/>
<p>The numbers that are added are called summands, the expression
1st addend + 2nd addend is called the sum and the result of the calculation is the value of the sum.</p>

<h2>Subtraction</h2>
<Image data="https://cdn.pixabay.com/photo/2018/04/03/21/07/abacus-3288079_960_720.jpg"/>
<p>The number from which is "subtracted" is called the minuend, the number which is "subtracted" is called the subtrahend.
The expression minuend - subtrahend is called difference and the result of the calculation is the value of the difference.</p>
